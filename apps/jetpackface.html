Description
Jetpack Face is a sidescroller, similar to games like Jetpack Joyride or Flappy Bird.
Tap with one finger to go slowly, two to go faster, and three to go EVEN FASTER!
Powerups include:
  • Clock - slows down time
  • Heart - gives you an extra health
  • Ice - allows you to collide with the ice blocks, and you get extra points for each block you hit
  • Fire - just like ice but with fire blocks
  • Ninja - automatically plays for you by throwing around shurikens
  • Laser - gives you a laser force field
  • Sunglasses smiley face - gives you four other powerups
  • Lightsaber - allows you to tap on a block to destroy it with the Force
