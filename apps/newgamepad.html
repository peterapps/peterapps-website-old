Description
NewGamepad was developed for exploring new-found joy for nostalgic platforms such as MAME, PSP, NDS and GBA.


It sports a huge array of platforms for all your gaming needs, all in one place. Play games for NDS, GBA, PSP, GBC, PS1, N64 and so much more. The developers of this app have consolidated all these emulators in one place, however they cannot guarantee their accuracy or reliability as it some of these emulators were developed by different, talented developers.
