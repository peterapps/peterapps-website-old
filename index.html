FAQ

Why is my device saying that it is unable to download the apps?

You haven't registered your UDID with Peter yet, which is sort of like a serial number. Send Peter your device's UDID at peterapps2016@gmail.com and tell him which app you want. It should be updated soon.

I already gave Peter my UDID, so why am I still unable to install apps?

Peter probably hasn't gotten around to uploading a new version of the app you want. Email him at peterapps2016@gmail.com and tell him which app you are trying to download. A new version should be uploaded soon.

How do you get all these apps and their info?

Peter scours the internet for useful apps, many of which come from iEmulators and Emu4iOS.

Why are there emulators here? Aren't emulators, like, illegal?

Emulators themselves are perfectly legal. However, downloading a pirated copy of a ROM violates copyright laws and is thus illegal, even if you own a physical copy of the game. Suffice it to say, Peter does not condone piracy or the illegal downloading of ROMs.
